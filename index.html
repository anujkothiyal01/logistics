About Us

Rylex Logistics (M) Sdn Bhd is the official business partner of J&T Express (Malaysia) Sdn Bhd responsible for the Last-Mile Parcel Delivery service representing J&T involving 20,000 parcels daily for the Klang and Shah Alam region.

The company has had exceptional growth within the logistics industry and is now geared to develop its First-Mile Delivery services within the region. With 200 staffs, 2 warehouses with a total size of 47,000 sqft and multiple land fleets ranging from 1 to 10 tonnes lorries, Rylex Logistics (M) Sdn Bhd is poised in the right direction for an exceptional market growth.
